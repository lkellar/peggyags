Peggy

Who is Peggy?

Peggy is a golden bowling pin that represents the best of Arkansas Governor's School.

A bowling pin?

A golden bowling pin, not just any standard bowling pin.

How can I meet Peggy?

Glad you asked! Peggy Club is held at the Baz Tech Patio, every Tuesday at 3:30. Make sure to be there on time, as you won't want to miss anything

How can I join Peggy Club?

It's easy! The only admission requirement is being an active participant of Arkansas Governor's School. Aside from that, there are no requirements.

Peggy Club is for everyone!
